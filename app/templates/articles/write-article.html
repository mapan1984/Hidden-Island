codehilite

shebang(with path)

#!/usr/bin/python
def hello(name):
    print("hello, %s" % name)

shebang(no path)

def hello(name):
    print("hello, %s" % name)

colons

def hello(name):
    print("hello, %s" % name)

日期格式

文章支持的日期格式为:

date: Thu Apr 27 21:36:25 CST 2017

它会被转换为Python datetime.date类型:

datetime.date(2017, 4, 27)
